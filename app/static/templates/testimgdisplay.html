Srcset & sizes with imgix

Using the imgix w, h, and dpr URL parameters, we can simplify the amount of effort it takes to generate the srcset attributes on our images. The numbers on the images are generated using imgix to give visual feedback on which image the browser has chosen to display based on the query.
